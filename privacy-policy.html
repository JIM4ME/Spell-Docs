Privacy Policy for SelfSpell

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Introduction

SelfSpell ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Audio Recordings: Voice recordings you create within the app are stored locally on your device only
  • User Goals: Text descriptions you enter for AI-generated affirmations

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device identifier for rate limiting purposes
  • Usage Statistics: Play counts, duration, and streak data stored locally on your device
  • Subscription Status: Managed by Apple's App Store

How We Use Your Information

Local Storage

  • All audio recordings are stored exclusively on your device
  • Usage statistics and preferences are stored locally
  • We do not have access to your recordings or personal data

AI Generation Service

When using the subscription-based AI generation feature:

  • Your text input is sent to our server
  • The server forwards it to third-party AI providers (Alibaba Cloud)
  • Generated content is returned to your device
  • We do not store your input or generated content on our servers

Rate Limiting

  • Device identifiers are used solely to prevent abuse
  • Request counts are stored temporarily (1 hour window)
  • No personal information is associated with these identifiers

Third-Party Services

Apple App Store

  • Subscription management is handled by Apple
  • Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

AI Providers (Subscription Mode)

  • Alibaba Cloud (Qwen)
  • Subject to their respective privacy policies
  • We recommend reviewing their policies if you use this feature

User API Key Mode

If you use your own API keys:

  • Requests go directly from your device to the AI provider
  • We have no access to your API keys or requests
  • Subject to the AI provider's privacy policy

Data Security

  • Audio recordings never leave your device
  • API keys are stored securely in your device's keychain
  • Server communications use HTTPS encryption
  • We implement rate limiting to prevent abuse

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your recordings through iOS file sharing
  • Opt out of AI features by not using them
  • Request information about data we process

Children's Privacy

Spell is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: SelfSpell@outlook.com
  • GitHub: https://github.com/JIM4ME/Spell-Docs

Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained until you delete the app
  • Server logs: Deleted after 7 days
  • Rate limiting data: Deleted after 1 hour

International Users

Spell is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of information to servers located in the United States and other countries.
